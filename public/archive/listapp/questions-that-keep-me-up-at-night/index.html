Questions That Keep Me Up at Night

Geoff Coffey
/ 9 June 2016

Inspired by @joemurphy

  • Am I ready to give up on this day?

  • I mean this is it. Once I sleep this day is gone.

  • One more day in a finite series.

  • Tick.

  • Tick.

  • It wasn't a bad day.

  • It was a great day.

  • But isn't there more I can get from it before I discard one thirty-thousandth of my life for good

  • Like when you get to the end of the toothpaste tube.

  • Maybe if I squeeze just a little more, scrape just a little harder, maybe there's one more thing I can extract from this day.

  • Or maybe not.

  • It's getting hard to focus.

  • But I'm not ready to say goodbye to this day forever.

  • Not yet.

  • Just one more...

  • One more...

  • I don't know what but there has to be more.

  • I'm not a quitter.

  • I want it all.

  • I'm never letting go.

  • Is this crazy?

  • This is probably crazy.

  • But it keeps me up at night.
