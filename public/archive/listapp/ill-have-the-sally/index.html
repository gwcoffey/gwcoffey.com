I'LL HAVE "THE SALLY"

Geoff Coffey
/ 10 December 2015

  • Salad

    Chef salad with the oil and vinegar on the side.

  • Pie

    Apple pie a la mode. But I'd like the pie heated. And I don't want the ice cream on top, I want it on the side. And I'd like strawberry instead of vanilla if you have it. If not then no ice cream, just whipped cream. But only if it's real. If it's out of a can then nothing.

  • Cocktail

    Regular tomato juice filled up about three quarters, then add a splash of Bloody Mary mix, just a splash. And a little piece of lime, but on the side.

  • Sandwich

    What she's having.

  • Second Pie

    Pecan.

  • Paprikash

    Easy on the pepper.

  • Cookie

    Mallowmars (for my man).

  • Cake

    Coconut, with the chocolate sauce on the side.
