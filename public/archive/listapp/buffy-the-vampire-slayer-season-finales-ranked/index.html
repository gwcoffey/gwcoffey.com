Buffy the Vampire Slayer Season Finales Ranked

Geoff Coffey
/ 15 October 2016

Best to worst.

  1. Season 5: The Gift

    "Remember: The ritual starts, we all die; and I'll kill anyone who comes near Dawn."

  2. Season 2: Becoming

    "I told him that I loved him... and I kissed him... and I killed him."

  3. Season 1: Prophecy Girl

    "I don't care. Giles, I'm sixteen years old. I don't want to die."

  4. Season 6: Grave

    "The thing is, yeah. I love you. I loved crayon-breaky Willow, and I love scary, veiny Willow. So if I'm going out, it's here. You wanna kill the world, well then start with me. I've earned that."

  5. Season 7: Chosen

    "She was incredible. She died saving my life."

  6. Season 4: Restless

    "I am a comfortador."

  7. Season 3: Graduation Day

    [I can't remember a single quote from this snore-fest.]
