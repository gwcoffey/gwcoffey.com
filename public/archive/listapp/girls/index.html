GIRLS

Geoff Coffey
/ 4 December 2015

  • The time in second grade when I just really wanted to kiss her and my teacher taught me about boundaries.

  • That time in fourth grade when Carrie said I was really funny and I fell madly in love with her because of it.

  • The time in fifth grade when Tiffany informed me we were going together for the day.

    And I was expected to kiss her in the back field at recess.

  • That time in sixth grade when Tina just started holding my hand at the skating rink and I was like "oh ok" and went with it.

  • That time in seventh grade when Tricia said her hands were cold and I warmed them up in my hands.

  • That time in eighth grade when Phyllis said "I like you" but she said it in a silly voice and I wasn't sure what to make of it so I never did anything.

  • That time in eighth grade when Nancy said I had a cute butt.

  • That time in eighth grade when Karen smiled at me after the teacher got me in trouble and it made me feel entirely better.

  • That time Freshman year when Jenn cried and I just wanted to make her feel better but I thought I would be a creep if I hugged her so I didn't.

  • The time Sophomore year when Nikki made fun of my poor spelling then said I was really really funny.
