WHO WOULD BE YOUR MANTOURAGE (FAMOUS DUDES TO HANG WITH)

Geoff Coffey
/ 12 January 2016

Who wants to join us? Thanks for the rec, @BWN_7! This was fun.

  • Cormac McCarthy

    Notoriously smart about all kinds of things. Also a handsome devil.

  • Charles Yu

    I'm entirely confident he and I would be buds because what he writes resonates with me deeply and the way he writes it thrills me.

  • Staya Nadella

    A soft spoken thoughtful smart man who happens to be CEO of a massive and massively important company is my kind of guy. Plus his smile reminds me of one of my best friends and it makes me want to cry.

  • Michael Silverblatt

    I would actually marry this guy just so we could talk every night.

  • Chiwetel Ejiofor

    I don't really know anything about the person but his performance is so spectacular that I'd love to hang with him anyway.

  • Art Speigelman

    Every time he opens his mouth my brain perks up. Brilliant insightful fun loving human.

  • Steve Buscemi

    To be fair I'd just expect him to be some of his characters and then be disappointed that he's probably just like another person but idk maybe he's cool.

  • Bill Gates

    Brilliant, deeply generous, notoriously cut-throat. What a combination!
