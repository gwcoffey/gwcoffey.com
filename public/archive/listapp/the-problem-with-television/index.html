The Problem with Television

Geoff Coffey
/ 14 August 2016

  • The problem with television is it all happens so fast.

  • Like I'm about to finally get around to watching West Wing.

  • Because everyone said it was really really good.

  • But we're past that now right?

  • Should I just skip right past Sopranos, Downton Abbey, and Game of Thrones...

  • ...And dive right in to Stranger Things or whatever it is called?

  • I guess what I mean is how do you get on this moving train without losing your legs?

  • If you would all stop talking about new things for a couple years I could catch up.

  • Then I'd be part of the conversation.

  • Deal?
