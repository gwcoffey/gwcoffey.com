First 10 thoughts that pass through my mind when I wake up

Geoff Coffey
/ 16 June 2016

Inspired by @bjnovak. I’m a deep sleeper.

  1. What am I?

  2. Am I alive?

  3. Who am I?

  4. Why do I exist?

  5. I vaguely recall a personal history.

  6. I think I am Geoff.

  7. It's starting to come back.

  8. Yes I remember now.

  9. I am Geoff. I have a life. I have a family. I have a job. I have a personal history.

  10. I should open my eyes.
