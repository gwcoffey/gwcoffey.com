Elementary Joys, 1985

Geoff Coffey
/ 19 May 2016

  • A warm ditto.

  • The funny smell of a mimeograph.

  • Being asked by the teacher to be in charge of advancing the film strip.

    *BEEP*

  • Getting a desk with the little pencil groove and a compartment under the top.

  • Parachute day in P.E.

  • Someone's mom brought cupcakes for their birthday.

  • Recess in the gym because it is raining.

  • CONVOCATION.

  • Listening to the school closure announcements on the radio when it is snowing.

  • Chocolate or vanilla milk? It's not vanilla, it's just milk. Whatever.
