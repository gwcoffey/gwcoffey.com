A LIST OF INTERESTING THINGS WELL EXPLAINED

Geoff Coffey
/ 27 May 2015

  • The singular Vi Hart’s beautiful three part exposition of the Fibonacci sequence, culminating in a clear explanation of why it shows up in plants. (Hint: It’s not magic.)

    Part 1: http://bit.ly/1dvB7rh | Part 2: http://bit.ly/1HuMpDk | Part 3: http://bit.ly/1dvB52M

  • This bygone film from Chevrolet explaining with incredible clarity the mechanics of the differential in your car.

    http://bit.ly/1Q8QAJE

  • The brilliant computer scientist Grace Hopper explains a nanosecond.

    http://bit.ly/1RlwQVQ

  • Preeminent physicist Richard Feynman explains what keeps trains on tracks.

    http://bit.ly/1HuMoiV

  • Author Susan Caine explains why introversion should be embraced rather than stigmatized.

    http://bit.ly/1CPZZib

  • Author John Green explains (ostensibly to teens) why reading matters, with sincerity and humor.

    http://bit.ly/1SAKjKY

  • Professor Moriarty from the University of Nottingham explains quantum tunneling.

    http://bit.ly/1PL9U54

  • Beloved astronomer Carl Sagan movingly explains why an astronomical perspective is anything but nihilistic.

    http://bit.ly/1dvB7ri
