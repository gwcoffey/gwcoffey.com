CHILDREN'S TELEVISION QUESTIONS

Geoff Coffey
/ 25 August 2015

  • What was up with Caillou's hair? Also what was up with Caillou?

  • So, can Ernie and Bert get married now?

  • You never really saw Emily Elizabeth with a giant plastic baggie.

  • Did anybody ever stop to think that maybe Oscar would engage with society in a more positive way if we provided him with a decent place to live? And stopped referring to him in a derogatory way? I'd be grouchy too...

  • Was The Electric Company anti-renewables propaganda?

  • Who's going to break it to Arthur that he's obviously not an aardvark?

  • Thomas the Train was the creepiest thing ever shown on television. It made Tales from the Crypt seem like the Hallmark after school special.

    This is not a question. It is a fact.

  • Why, if this is the land of make-believe where you can literally make up whatever you want, did you make Lady Fairchild such a meanie? Was make believe not real enough or something?

  • Was the central theme of Angelina Ballerina that even if you're a little bit stupid you can still be successful in an artsy way?

  • Was there ever anything as cool as dinosaurs or as lame as Barney? This is a contradiction.

  • Speaking of Mr. Rogers, he really could hear me, right? I mean he asked questions and I answered and he praised me and IT WAS REAL RIGHT?

  • Nine Pumpkin Pies: Best Sesame Street skit or best skit period?
