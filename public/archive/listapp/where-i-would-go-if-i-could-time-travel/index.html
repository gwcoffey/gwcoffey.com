WHERE I WOULD GO IF I COULD TIME TRAVEL?

Geoff Coffey
/ 7 September 2015

#4 of my last minute sharing week dump. Inspired by @ChrisK

  • 1978 Mesa Arizona

    Because nothing would make me happier than seeing my wife as a toddler being adorable.

  • The very distant future

    Because I want to know about our future, what becomes of us. Because it is more unknowable than the past. Because we will learn things in the next, say, 1,000 years and I'll never know them and that makes me a little sad.

  • Turn of the century America

    Because I think it is more like today than we realize and I wonder what this period of yellow journalism, jingoistic tendencies, reevaluation of American values, and post-major-recession economy felt like.

  • My teenage years

    To tell myself it would be ok and it is ok to believe in things.
