Things I Like More Than Pokémon Go

Geoff Coffey
/ 13 July 2016

I don’t even have the app because I am an inept gamer who doesn’t get it most of the time. What do you do with a Pokémon once you catch it? Nobody can explain this to me.

  • Watching people play Pokémon Go around the neighborhood.

  • Hearing people excitedly talk about Pokémon Go at the grocery store.

  • Seeing people post to social media about the joy they get from Pokémon Go.

  • Hearing my friend say this about his daughter's relationship with Pokémon Go: "Let me put it this way. I told her it was her turn to walk the dog and she jumped right up without complaining."

  • Reading lists about Pokémon Go.

  • Watching Nintendo's stock price respond positively to the wonderful reaction to Pokémon Go.

    And imagining that this will bolster their newly-found realization that they need to bring their tremendous talent to mobile gaming.

  • Hearing luddites turn up the dial on their "this is what's wrong with the world" nonsense.

  • Imagining the Pokémon-obsessed kids in my life having so much fun.

  • Hearing confused moms cheer their children on.

  • Go Pokémon, go...
