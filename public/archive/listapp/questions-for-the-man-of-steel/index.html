QUESTIONS FOR THE MAN OF STEEL

Geoff Coffey
/ 7 February 2016

  • Does it hurt when this happens to you?

  • Does this leave a bruise?

  • Is this more painful than the above, or less, or the same?

  • When you destroy a city with your wrecking ball body, what is worse, the physical discomfort or the guilt?

  • Is this imperiling you in any way?

  • When you kiss Amy Adams do you have to consciously make your lips soft so you don't break her face?

  • Does it count as self-sacrifice if you are invincible?
