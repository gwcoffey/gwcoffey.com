THINGS I'D DO TO TRY AND CHEER YOU UP ☀️

Geoff Coffey
/ 31 December 2015

Inspired by @ListPrompts. Age-dependent obv.

  • ~~~ 10 years old and younger ~~~

  • Act like I tripped, fall on the floor, flail a bit, ask for your help getting up. Do this with lots of absurd flair.

    Did this all the time with my kids. Worked like a champ.

  • Kiss you all over the head.

  • Listen and then tell you I understand.

  • Start a sword fight with serving spoons.

  • ~~~ 11 to 18 years old ~~~

  • Give you space.

  • Do your job for you and tell you you got a pass today.

  • Make your favorite easy dinner, buy you an orange crush, and serve it to you in your bedroom.

  • Possibly write a note on the paper napkin along with the above.

  • Sing you a Very Meaningful Song (tm) like Dylan's Make You Feel My Love and then transition half way through to I Feel Pretty while wearing a flowered hat.

  • ~~~ Adults ~~~

  • Darned if I know. Adults are hard to figure out. I'll bake you an awesome treat but it won't end up helping.
