THINGS I'M NOT ALLERGIC TO

Geoff Coffey
/ 26 August 2015

This is a partial list. Full disclosure: I am mildly allergic to formaldehyde and chlorine. Swimming pools make me feel kind of gross for a few hours.

  • Latex

  • Any medication

  • Bee stings

  • Tree nuts

  • Shellfish

  • Peanuts

  • Wheat

    Please. I'd rather be dead.

  • Egg

  • Dairy

  • Soy

  • Gluten

  • Dust

  • Pet Dander

  • Strawberries

    My poor wife.

  • Pollen

    Except one spring maybe 8 years ago I had some crazy allergy thing where my nose was running constantly and my eyes itched. It lasted like a month and then never came back. Man life sucks for you allergy people.

  • Poison Ivy

    My claim to fame in elementary school. I had a reaction once and then never again. I can roll in the stuff. Actually I could make a whole list of things that are bad for most people but mild for me.
