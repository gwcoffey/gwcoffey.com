THE TWENTY BEST MOMENTS IN STAR WARS

Geoff Coffey
/ 29 December 2015

SPOILERS near the end I guess. Not really but sort of.

  1. "I want to learn the ways of the force and become a Jedi like my father."

  2. "It's a boring conversation anyway."

  3. "This is some rescue."

  4. "You're all clear kid. Now let's blow this thing and go home."

  5. "*Admiral* Piett."

  6. "I cannot teach him."

  7. "That is why you fail."

  8. "I'm nice men."

  9. "I'm not a committee!"

  10. "I am altering the deal. Pray I don't alter it anymore."

  11. "I know."

    Actually the best line in the whole series.

  12. "No, I am your father."

  13. "Unexpected this is. And unfortunate."

  14. "You will find it is you who are mistaken— about a great many things."

  15. "So be it, Jedi."

  16. "You were right. You were right about me. Tell your sister you were right."

  17. "The garbage will do!"

  18. "Chewie, we're home."

  19. "Yes, anything."
