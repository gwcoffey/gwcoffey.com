WHAT I WOULD DO IF I HAD A TIME MACHINE

Geoff Coffey
/ 23 July 2015

  • Revisit my most painful memory.

  • Kill the mother of my mortal enemy.

  • Kill my mortal enemy as a child.

  • Save Buckbeak.

  • Dress up as a Connecticut Yankee and visit king Arthur's court.

  • Destroy It.

  • Tour a Tralfamadorian zoo.

  • Buy a sports almanac and save my mother.
