READING IN TO HARPER LEE

Geoff Coffey
/ 19 February 2016

  • From Go Set a Watchman:

  • "If you did not want much, there was plenty."

  • I'm not sure if that was meant to be an epicurean admonishment to not want so much, or a dig at the rural south for not having much to offer..

  • ...but I choose the former interpretation.

  • And say thank you to Harper Lee for giving us so much in so few volumes.
