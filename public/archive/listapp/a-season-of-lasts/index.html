A Season of Lasts

Geoff Coffey
/ 20 May 2016

  • Last school concert I attend to watch Isabel.

  • Last donation check I write to her school.

  • Last parent teacher conference with Isabel.

  • Last awards ceremony.

  • Last time I make her signature sausage and egg sandwich for finals week breakfast.

  • Last time I write her a cheesy napkin poem and tuck it in her lunch.

  • Last time I see her in her school uniform.

  • First time my baby rules the world.
