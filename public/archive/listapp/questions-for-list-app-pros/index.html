QUESTIONS FOR LIST APP PROS

Geoff Coffey
/ 26 May 2015

  • What's up with list title input when it puts things you did not type all around your insertion point?

  • Who agrees that we should have a "reverse numerical" list type?

  • Is there anywhere to see a person's account name?

  • Can I get a notification when someone comments on a list I commented on?

  • How many List App users are there?

  • How far back in someone's list history can you read, like and share before it becomes creepy? Does gender play a role? Age?
