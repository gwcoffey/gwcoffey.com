THINGS I SAID TO MY DAUGHTER TO TALK HER OUT OF MAKING ME TAKE HER TO MINIONS

Geoff Coffey
/ 4 August 2015

I have now seen minions but I did not want to.

  • We could rent something!

    SOPHIA: I think we should get out of the house!

  • Ooh. Jurassic Park has dinosaurs. Could be good?

    SOPHIA: Yeah but it doesn't start til 8.

  • We could see Mission Impossible! Let's watch the preview for mission impossible!

    SOPHIA: That does look good. ... I hate making decisions. How do I decide?

  • Well Minions has a Rotten tomato rating of 54%. That's a terrible F. Mission Impossible has a 93% which is a pretty decent A.

    SOPHIA: But Minions has Minions and they are really cute.

  • Maybe it's a renter! We can see Mission Impossible on the BIG SCREEN and rent minions when it comes out.

    SOPHIA: Yeah but if minions are cute then BIG SCREEN minions are cuter!

  • FINE.

    SOPHIA: yay
