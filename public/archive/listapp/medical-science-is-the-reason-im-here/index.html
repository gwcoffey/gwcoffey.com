Geoff Coffey
/ 26 October 2015

I’ll put the people I love. You add you!

  • My brother in law: lymphoma at age 35

  • My brother in law: two broken vertebrae in the neck

  • My business partner: spinal cord injury, severe internal bleeding, multiple organ failure
