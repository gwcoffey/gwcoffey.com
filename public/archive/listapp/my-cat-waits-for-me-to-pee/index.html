My Cat Waits for Me to Pee

Geoff Coffey
/ 18 October 2016

  • My cat waits for me to pee.

  • He waits very patiently.

  • At my feet.

  • And when I stand he stands.

  • And looks up excitedly.

  • I flush.

  • And he jumps up on the toilet seat.

  • He watches the water swirl.

  • With obvious feline anticipation.

  • And then he drinks.

  • A long careful deliberate drink.

  • Every time.

  • But only when I use the guest bathroom.

  • He has a water bowl.

  • This is weird right?
