Should Belle Marry Beast: Pros and Cons

Geoff Coffey
/ 1 October 2016

  • CON: Total jerk.

  • CON: Imprisoned her for life for no reason.

  • CON: Can't read.

  • CON: Can't use a spoon.

  • CON: Seems to be mixed up in some kind of dark magic.

  • CON: Uses threats of violence and withholds food to coerce her into dating him.

  • CON: Mean to beautiful enchantresses disguised as old beggar women.

  • CON: Is some kind of talking animal.

  • CON: Pretty ugly.

  • CON: Not very smart.

  • CON: Lives in the middle of nowhere and insists she live with him.

  • CON: Probably not a very tender lover.

  • CON: They have nothing in common.

  • PRO: Has a killer library.
