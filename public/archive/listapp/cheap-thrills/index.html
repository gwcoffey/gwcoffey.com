CHEAP THRILLS

Geoff Coffey
/ 19 February 2016

Inspired by @semioccasional by way of @biz

  • Reading @imc's poetry.

    Perennial winner of the high school poetry contest, poet laureate of the school, and she sometimes lets me brag.

  • Giving @sophster her first Bomb Pop yesterday.

    Somehow she's 14 and has never had them before. I failed as a father somewhere along the way. Also, only 40 calories?! I'll take 10 please.

  • Running a suite of unit tests after a big refactor.

  • Embarrassing but if we're being honest here:

  • Filling up my circles.

  • Seeing Pluto up close for the first time after imagining it all my life.

  • Baking. The closest thing to real magic.

  • Looking up at night.

  • Chamomile tea. It's just dried flowers (?!), but it warms me up, tastes so good, and makes me happy.

  • Being smiled at when you're nervous.
