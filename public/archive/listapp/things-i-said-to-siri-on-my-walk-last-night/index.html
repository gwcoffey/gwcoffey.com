THINGS I SAID TO SIRI ON MY WALK LAST NIGHT

Geoff Coffey
/ 2 July 2015

The perks and perils of growing your music library from 6,000 to 30,000,000 songs.

  • Hey Siri play the song You Can't Hold Back Spring

    …what is this crap?!

  • Hey Siri play the Tom Waits song You Can't Hold Back Spring

    …wait what?

  • Hey Siri play the song You Can't Hold Back Spring by Tom Waits

    …ahhh, that's the stuff.

  • Hey Siri play the song Mexican Home by John Prine

    …oh my word no not the twangy one.

  • Hey Siri play Mexican Home *Live* by John Prine

    ...ahhh that's the stuff.

  • Hey Siri play the song Remember When by…um…Robert? Jackson

    …whoa stop stop stop.

  • Hey Siri play the song Remember When by Jackson

    …oh *Allen* Jackson. Thanks Siri! That's the stuff. (What can I say, I miss my wife and when I miss my wife my sentimentality shields are down to 20%.)

  • [hey this could be cool] Hey Siri play the music from Me and Earl and the Dying Girl

    …what? No.

  • Hey Siri play that Brian Eno music from Me and Earl and the Dying Girl

    ...Darn. I guess you don't have that.

  • Hey Siri play some Iris Dement I don't own

    …no? Darn.

  • Hey Siri play that song from silver linings playbook

    ...hey cool. That's the one!

  • Hey Siri play hits from 1993

    ...holy nostalgia.
