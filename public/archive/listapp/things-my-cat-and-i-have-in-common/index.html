THINGS MY CAT AND I HAVE IN COMMON

Geoff Coffey
/ 8 August 2015

  • Would prefer to stay home

  • Mammal

  • Fond of crunchy food

  • Covered entirely or partially with hair

  • Turns nose up to mushy foods from a can

  • Happy to hide in the back room when people come over

  • Can sleep anywhere and in any position

  • Not really that in to catnip

  • Prefer most things to unplanned doctor visits

  • Pretty tidy

  • Currently shedding a bit

    Seriously guys, what's up with my hair? Hairs everywhere. On my desk. On my keyboard. On my shirt. In the sink. Is this what it feels like to go bald?

  • Simple needs

  • Loves to be alone for a little while but then starts to slowly go insane if home alone for days and days

  • Poops in a box

    Just kidding about this one.

  • Often feels like vomiting but never actually vomits
