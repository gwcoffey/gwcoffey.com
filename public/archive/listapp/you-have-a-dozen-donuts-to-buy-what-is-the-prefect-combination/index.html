YOU HAVE A DOZEN DONUTS TO BUY, WHAT IS THE PREFECT COMBINATION?

Geoff Coffey
/ 21 July 2016

Inspired by @wallflower, who I respect immensely but she is misguided. From Rainbow Doughnuts in north phoenix (not the same as rainbow doughnuts in east phoenix).

  • 5 glazed raised.

    The best overall doughnut.

  • 2 apple fritters.

    The best imposter doughnut.

  • 2 glazed buttermilk.

    So good.

  • 2 chocolate buttermilk.

    Ditto.

  • 2 Boston cream.

    But only if it's pastry cream inside. If it's frosting-filled, cancel your order and burn the place down.

  • (Yes this is 13 because that's what Rainbow calls a dozen.)
