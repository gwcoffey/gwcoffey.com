TIMES MY FAMILY HAS PURCHASED "WHEN HARRY MET SALLY"

Geoff Coffey
/ 1 January 2016

I’ve paid my dues.

  • 1991: I bought a VHS copy in high school so I didn't have to rent it anymore.

  • 1992: My wife (then girlfriend) bought a VHS copy once I indoctrinated her.

    These two lived side by side on the shelf when we got married.

  • 2000: We bought it on DVD when we got a DVD player.

  • [2010: My DVD player died and I didn't really want to deal with disks anymore anyway so I ripped a few of my favorite DVDs, including this one, and put them on my network backup drive.]

  • 2015: I got lazy and bought the iTunes digital download last night.

    I can get out my laptop, load up the movie, and then stream it to my TV but ugh...now I can just click play with my Apple TV remote without having to get off the couch, the way God intended.
