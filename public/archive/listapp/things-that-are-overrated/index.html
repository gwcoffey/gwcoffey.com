THINGS THAT ARE OVERRATED

Geoff Coffey
/ 28 May 2015

Time to make some enemies on List App.

  • In n Out

    I totally don't get it. Better than McDonald's? Sure, but so's my cat's litter box. Good? Eh, in a pinch I guess. Great? Not even close. Lousy fries, unseasoned meat, inexcusably bad cheese, poor shakes.

  • That batman movie with Heath Ledger

    Any batman movie really (oh man that voice just makes me laugh and laugh) but especially this one which people seem to think is AHMAZINGGGG. The only good thing I ever saw in a batman movie was Marion Cotillard.

  • My Apple Watch

    I like it, and if I lost it next month I'd probably replace it since it is handy in a dozen little ways. But it hasn't once made me pancakes or whatever.

  • Krispy Kreme

    Too greasy and way Way WAY too much saccharine-sweet glaze. And besides, who wants a hot doughnut? So much better room temperature.

  • Any barbecue in Phoenix except Little Miss BBQ

    Bobby-Q? Barf. Famous Dave's? Please. Little Miss BBQ? My body is ready.
