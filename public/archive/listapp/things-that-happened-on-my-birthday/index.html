THINGS THAT HAPPENED ON MY BIRTHDAY

Geoff Coffey
/ 21 July 2016

  • 1649: Elie Wiesel's fictional trial of God takes place.

  • 1713: Frederick the First of Prussia, grandfather of Frederick the Great, dies.

  • 1759: Authorities attempt to seize all printed copies of Voltaire's Candide in Paris on the grounds that it is seditious.

  • 1836: The patent for the Colt revolver pistol is granted.

  • 1870: The first African American senator is sworn in.

  • 1932: Adolf Hitler becomes a German citizen.

  • 1952: A solar eclipse is visible in Europe.

  • 1959: The California Vehicle Code is reorganized and adopted.

  • 1968: Hundreds of unarmed North Vietnamese citizens are murdered in the HÃ  My Massacre.

  • 1965: The insufferable and oddly muscular Carrot Top is born.

  • 1966: Alexis Denisof of Buffy the Vampire Slayer fame is born.

  • 1966: Tea Leoni is born.

  • 1971: Sean Astin of Goonies and Lord of the Rings fame is born.

  • 1975: I am born.

  • 1975: Drew Barrymore turns three days old.

    (I know you think this is nothing but this is something.)

  • 1975: The West German Constitutional Court criminalizes abortion without justifying indications.

  • 1976: Rashida Jones, arguably the most beautiful person on earth, is born.
