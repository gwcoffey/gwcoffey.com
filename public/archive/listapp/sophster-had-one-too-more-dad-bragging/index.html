@sophster Had One Too [more dad bragging]

Geoff Coffey
/ 21 May 2016

Maybe this is just a thing schools do now because @sophster goes to a totally different school from @imc and her “8th Grade Promotion” included a personal message/tribute to each kid too! Once again these lovely lovely teachers spoke truth.

  • "As teachers we could only hope for more students like Sophia:"

  • "Vibrant, full of energy, always present, with a welcoming smile or a high five..."

    [Standing on stage awkwardly, not sure what to do with herself during this tribute, Sophie at this point raises her hand for a high five from Mrs. Frerichs the middle school principal. She obliges. The audience laughs.]

  • "...Sophia can't help but make an impression."

  • "Her tech-savvy, wit, and passion for her cats only scratches [pause for laughter] the surface of the depth of knowledge and heart that flows through her."

  • "I have little doubt that Sophia will excel in high school."

  • "With her 1.21 gigawatts of energy and heart..."

  • "she is a shining example of what a person can be."

  • ...

  • My gosh this particular end-of-the-school-year is killing me.
