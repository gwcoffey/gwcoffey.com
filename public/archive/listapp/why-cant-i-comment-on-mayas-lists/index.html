WHY CAN'T I COMMENT ON @MAYA'S LISTS

Geoff Coffey
/ 5 October 2015

  • Does this mean I'm blocked?

    It's ok if I am. No hard feelings. But like there's also someone named @olive that I can't see at all so I assumed that is what happens when you're blocked. I can see Maya's lovely lists but I just don't get a comment button.

  • Is there a setting that only allows comments from someone who follows you?

    Maybe @maya has it set up that way?

  • Guys this is important because I'm like the only Mormon on list app and I need give her advice on how to get some of those skittles.

    Scripture references to toss around. Cultural oddities to worm your way into their heart. Maybe even use the word "ponderize" which is like super timely and hip in Mormon circles right now.
