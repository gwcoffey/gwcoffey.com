REASONS I HAVEN'T LISTED LATELY

Geoff Coffey
/ 2 October 2015

  • Sophia's in a play and has rehearsal in Scottsdale Monday-Thursday and I take her two days a week.

    Zoom zoom to the school at 3:00 to pick her up, zoom home, eat, homework, zoom to Scottsdale, drop her off, zoom to restaurant with wifi, work for 2 hours 45 minutes, zoom to pick her up, zoom home. Home by 9:00. Zoom zoom.

  • Isabel is in a school play.

    Practice practice. I love to help her practice.

  • Both girls are now doing voice lessons.

    I'm not sure how this happened. Sophia doesn't even like to sing. She needs *a lot* of practice help.

  • My wife is working full time now.

    This makes me happy because she loves her new job so much. And she is amazing at it.

  • My mamma had major surgery.

    And my poor daddio was a wreck.

  • I still work duh.

    Bless you flexible work hours.

  • Church stuff you know? Mormons be busy.

  • Oh my gosh guys I'm out of time all the time.

  • I gave up on my nightly 4 mile walk for now.

    This is the only sad part of this list.

  • But it's ok because life is beautiful and the craziness will pass.

    Some day I'll be a little sad I don't have all this kid stuff to do.
