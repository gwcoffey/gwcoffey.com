BEST PARTS OF THE HOBBIT MOVIES

Geoff Coffey
/ 31 October 2015

  • When Legolas dances with that troll guy. So elegant.

  • When Kate from Lost smirks like she knows it's all a dream.

  • When the dragon isn't Sean Connery.

  • How Bard kind of looks like Legolas from Pirates of the Carribean.

  • When the guy turns in to a bear and you're like huh, I never would have guessed he would be a bear until all the hints.

  • When the physical comedy stops happening for a few seconds a every few hours.

  • Bird poop. Lol.
