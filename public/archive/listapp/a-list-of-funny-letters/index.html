A LIST OF FUNNY LETTERS

Geoff Coffey
/ 24 May 2015

  1. Letter from Tom Hanks to the producers of the "Nerdist" podcast cursing their ingenuity in securing him as a guest.

    http://bit.ly/1c6L1hm

  2. Letter from Raymond Chandler parodying the Science Fiction genre and inadvertently inventing the word "Google".

    http://bit.ly/1SgCUAj

  3. Memo to all staff at NPR about the imminent arrival of something called "The Internet" at their offices.

    http://bit.ly/1Kndgr3

  4. Letter from Mark Twain to the next burglar in his house.

    http://bit.ly/1c6OtIT

  5. Letter from Walt Disney to Walter Lantz, creator of Woody Woodpecker on behalf of a fan who thought the woodpecker was a Disney character.

    http://bit.ly/1GrwPMI

  6. Letter from Stanley Kubrick verifying that IBM, which will be credited as a consulting company in "2001: A Space Odyssey", is aware of the plot specifics vis-a-vis HAL.

    http://bit.ly/1HjmApL

  7. Letter from E. B. White to the American Society for the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals regarding dog bureaucracy.

    http://bit.ly/1F3j7v8

  8. Apology letter from Sly and the Family Stone to Dartmouth College.

    http://bit.ly/1Hx7Itn

  9. Collection of tone-deaf memos from the president of Tiger Oil Company to all employees.

    http://bit.ly/1GrpPzn

  10. Reply from Ronald Reagan to a young fan upon the declaration of his bedroom as a "disaster area" by his mother.

    http://bit.ly/1SgOqvs
