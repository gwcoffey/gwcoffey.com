The Five Senses

Geoff Coffey
/ 23 June 2017

  1. The one where you know someone is behind you.

  2. The one where you know a friend has had a bad day.

  3. The one where you're forgetting something and you know it but you have no idea what it is.

  4. The one where you know you're getting sick even though you have no symptoms.

  5. The one where you're holding a flimsy full plastic drink cup and you know it's on the verge of slipping even though it hasn't slipped yet.
