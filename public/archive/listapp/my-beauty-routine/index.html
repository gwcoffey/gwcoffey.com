MY BEAUTY ROUTINE

Geoff Coffey
/ 17 April 2016

  • I shower every day.

    Twice most days in the summer.

  • I brush my hair kind of quickly.

    I used to use hair spray but maybe 18 months ago I ran out as I was leaving for a trip and I realized I don't actually need it. So I stopped using it. Look how dashing my hair is anyway.

  • I shave. I don't have much of a beard though so I skip this step a lot.

  • As far as products I use soap and shampoo. I particularly like the green kind.

  • My barber trims my eyebrows.

  • That's about it. I'm beautiful.
