QUESTIONS FOR GOD

Geoff Coffey
/ 30 March 2016

  • Was it worth it making men and women different?

  • How come when I bite my lip it gets sore and bigger so it is easy to bite again and hurts a lot when I do?

  • Do chickens know they're delicious? Does it give them pride, or terror?

  • Did you make it beautiful or did you just make us see it that way?

  • When you tipped the earth on its axis were you precise about it or did you just sort of lean it over and spin?

  • In order for me to have all the stuff I had, a lot of people had to suffer. This isn't a question. I just felt like I should tell someone.

  • Can you say things in your language that have no translation to English? Is that what it means to feel?

  • Did you mean for clouds to happen or did they just sort of happen once you did the water and the sky and stuff?

  • Were you a little boy/girl God when you made dinosaurs because when I was a boy that's totally what I would have created if I had that kind of power.

  • Have you ever been alone?

  • Thank you for your time. You were good to me.
