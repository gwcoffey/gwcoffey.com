PEOPLE IN AMERICA WHO DIED FROM GUNS YESTERDAY

Geoff Coffey
/ 19 June 2015

Statistically if it’s like a typical day, 65% suicide, 30% homicide, 5% accidental.

  • 9 people in Charleston

    The media and social media will obsess over these nine people for a few days.

  • Approximately 65 other people

    If they were ever mentioned they're already forgotten by everyone but their loved ones because they died individually instead of in a group.

  • Bonus item: Approximately 75 more will die today

    So it goes.
