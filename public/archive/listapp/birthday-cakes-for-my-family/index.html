BIRTHDAY "CAKES" FOR MY FAMILY

Geoff Coffey
/ 11 December 2015

I love to bake…

  • Sophia always asks for pineapple upside down cake.

    So delicious and perfect for December.

  • Isabel (@imc) has a summer birthday and usually wants fresh berry tart with pastry cream.

    Delicious AND beautiful. Her birthday is in July so we usually have a red/white/blue thing going on with blanched blueberries and strawberries or raspberries.

  • My wife is trickier. Her tastes vary. Sometimes pumpkin cheesecake. Sometimes flourless chocolate cake. Sometimes chocolate cream pie. Sometimes creme brûlée.

    The only hard part is getting her to say what she wants. I usually just surprise her.

  • And for me? Cookies and ice cream.

    There's a great cookie bakery around the corner so I can buy this and it is amazing.
