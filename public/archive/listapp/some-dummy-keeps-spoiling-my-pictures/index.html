SOME DUMMY KEEPS SPOILING MY PICTURES

Geoff Coffey
/ 23 March 2016

I’ve been spending a couple lovely vacation days alone seeing the sights.

  1. Tried to take a picture of this Boojum tree but some dummy was standing in the way.

  2. Tried to get a picture of this pretty pink splash of flowers but some dummy swooped in from out of nowhere.

  3. A lovely mesquite with some dummy standing in the way playing with his watch.

  4. This thing's been in the lobby of the Phoenix Art Museum for years. That dummy's only been there for a minute.

  5. Michelangelo in San Lorenzo. Some dummy in my way.

  6. A picture of some dummy taking a picture of a picture of a library.

  7. This is one of my favorite pieces at the Phoenix Art museum. View slightly obstructed by some dummy.

  8. Some dummy spoiling the effect in the firefly room.

  9. This transcendent bronze sculpture provides a striking inspiration for some dummy.

  10. Tried to get a fry bread taco but some dummy is eating it.
