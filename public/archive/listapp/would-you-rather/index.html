WOULD YOU RATHER...

Geoff Coffey
/ 4 March 2016

  • Never touch another person again.

    Or never speak to another person again.

  • Never use google again.

    Or never read a book again.

  • Give up your favorite food forever.

    Or eat only your current favorite food for dinner for the rest of your life.

  • Have Bill Gates' wealth.

    Or have basic necessities for life.

  • Die by being eaten by a lion.

    Or die of starvation.

  • Hand Donald Trump the Gold Codes.

    Or be burned at the stake today. (Honestly this one's not even hard.)
