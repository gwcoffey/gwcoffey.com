THE ONLY THING WORSE THAN BEN AFLEK AS BATMAN...

Geoff Coffey
/ 26 March 2016

Sorry for being so judgey.

  • Is batman.

  • Why does he talk that way?

  • Am I supposed to identify with him?

  • Am I supposed to sympathize with him?

  • Am I supposed to empathize with him?

  • Because I don't do any of those things.

  • Mostly because he sounds so stupid when he talks.

  • That voice...how do you not just laugh at how ridiculous it is?

  • The only good thing about batman is...

  • The bat dad YouTube series...

  • And Marion Cotillard.

  • She is very beautiful.

  • Note:

  • I have only seen two batman movies...

  • The one everybody went crazy for with the Joker guy,

  • And the really insufferably awful one that made no sense with the guy with the scuba mask on his face.

  • So I've never actually seen Ban Aflek as batman.

  • Maybe he doesn't talk in that stupid way.
