THIS OR THAT: BOOK LOVERS EDITION

Geoff Coffey
/ 7 April 2016

Inspired by @jaidub

  • Physical Book or eBook?

    eBook. Unless it is not available, or the book format works better on paper. But I much prefer e.

  • Audio book or book in hand?

    Both. I can't pay attention to an audio book very well so all my serious reading is book-in-hand but I do listen to audio books a lot, usually things I've already read, and especially things that benefit from good readers like Shakespeare or Joyce.

  • Paperback or hardcover?

    Never thought much about it but I'd never pay extra just for format unless it was somehow special.

  • Adult or young adult?

    Adult.

  • Series or standalone?

    Standalone mostly but I'm not averse to series. Border Trilogy by Cormac McCarthy was my last series I think.

  • Dog ears or bookmarks?

    My kindle takes care of this for me.

  • Bookstores or online?

    Online for eBooks, book store for paper.

  • Fiction or non-fiction?

    Probably 80% fiction, 20% non-fiction.

  • Monster read or short and sweet?

    Either. Both. Why not both?

  • Starry-eyed romance or action-packed?

    Not very relevant to my selection process. I like all kinds of things but if I had to pick a style it wood be wordy, rambling, and cerebral.

  • Curl up inside or bathe in the sun?

    Inside. I'm sun-averse.
