THINGS I'M EMBARASSED TO ADMIT MADE ME CRY

Geoff Coffey
/ 19 May 2015

I’m sentimentally challenged which made reading to my girls difficult.

  • When Beezus Quimby's mother tells her she and her sister didn't get along either when they were little.

  • When Miri finds out why her father won't let her work in the quarry in Princess Academy

  • When Charlotte says by helping Wilbur perhaps she was trying to lift up her own life a trifle.

  • When Hermione tells Harry he's a great wizard

  • When Katniss volunteers as tribute

  • When Sophie realizes she's not real in Sophie's World

  • When Rooster carries Mattie in his arms in True Grit

  • The end of Mean Girls
