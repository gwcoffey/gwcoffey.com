TRUMP'S CHANCES

Geoff Coffey
/ 1 March 2016

Maybe I’m just an optimist but this is my read.

  • Chances he will win the most delegates: High

  • Chances he will receive the nomination: Medium

  • Chances he will build consensus support among Republicans: Low

  • Chances he will win the presidency: Zero

  • Chances he will suffer the greatest electoral defeat since Mondale: Hopeful
