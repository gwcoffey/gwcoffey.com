IF THE POWERBALL DOUBLES ONE MORE TIME...

Geoff Coffey
/ 11 January 2016

  • Then for the first time ever it would be a winning move to buy every possible ticket.

  • This will cost you about $600 million.

  • But your after tax winnings will be more than $600 million.

  • Except...

  • If someone else wins too you have to share.

  • And then you'd be out $300 million.

  • So don't buy every ticket.

  • It's not actually a winning move.
