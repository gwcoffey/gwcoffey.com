THINGS THAT ARE HARD WITH A BANDAID ON YOUR THUMB

Geoff Coffey
/ 17 January 2016

The tale of a surprisingly debilitating injury.

  • My cat attacked my thumb and "scratched" it so deeply it bled profusely and I had to put on a bandaid. It is much more a deep cut than a scratch. I did not know those little monsters could do that.

    Say hi Sadness.

  • I don't think I've had a bandaid on my thumb in the last, maybe, ten years.

  • It is annoying.

  • These are the things that are hard in order of severity:

  • Unlocking my iPhone.

  • Typing on my iPhone.

  • Tying my tie.

  • Hand-washing some dishes.

  • [there are probably more but I'm sick of typing with one finger]
