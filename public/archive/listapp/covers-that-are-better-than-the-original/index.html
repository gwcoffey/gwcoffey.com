COVERS THAT ARE BETTER THAN THE ORIGINAL➕

Geoff Coffey
/ 22 May 2015

  • Bridge Over Troubled Water / Johnny Cash

    I never liked this song until I heard Cash's version. Now it moves me deeply. http://bit.ly/1GstLQy

  • Every Grain of Sand / Emmy Lou Harris

    I loved the original but Emmy's is more haunting. http://bit.ly/1GsBv54

  • Can't Help Falling in Love / U2

    Sublime take on an otherwise uninteresting song. http://bit.ly/1R9TsbL

  • Clay Pigeons / John Prine

    The Blaze Foley original is great. The Prine version is better. http://bit.ly/1KoTQ5j

  • Knockin' on Heavens Door / Dave Grisman and Jerry Garcia

    This is probably blasphemy but I never though much of this song until I caught this Grisman/Garcia version on iTunes Radio. http://bit.ly/1GtOmnv

  • My Mexican Home / John Prine

    This is a cheat but the original studio release of this song is twangy and forgettable. A rare miss for Prine. But the reworked live version, finally released on the "In Person & On Satge" album is brooding, powerful, and sad and one of my favorites. http://bit.ly/1Hxb6o7
