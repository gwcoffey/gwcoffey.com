Thanksgiving

Geoff Coffey
/ 25 November 2016

  • Turkey

    With discreet lemon half because it kept leaning to the right.

  • Sausage cornbread dressing.

    My personal favorite.

  • Green bean casserole.

    All from scratch because that's the way I roll.

  • 3-to-1 mashed potatoes.

    Six pounds of russets, two pounds of European butter.

  • Potato leek soup

    With homemade créme fraîche. Made special for @sophster the vegetarian.

  • Some bread.

    First time trying the recipe. Needs more salt. And I need to up my braiding game. But still tasty.

  • My special skillet corn bread.

    Another of my favorites. So decadent and delicious.

  • Cranberry salad with pears and ginger.

    @mamiecoffey's favorite.

  • @sophster's impeccable deep dish apple pie.

    She is the pie whisperer. I have no idea how she always gets her crust so flaky and perfect.

  • Pumpkin cheesecake.

    Mmmmm.

  • Almond pistachio torrone.

    For my mommy but I made a little extra for us too.
