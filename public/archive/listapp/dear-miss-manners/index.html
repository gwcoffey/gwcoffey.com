Dear Miss Manners

Geoff Coffey
/ 5 May 2016

  • Is it wrong to put a little smear of soft butter on this cookie before I eat it?

  • Ok but I mean is it *really* wrong?

  • Like on a scale of picking-a-difficult-booger-when-nobody's-around to voting-for-Donald-Trump, how wrong is it?

  • It's pretty low on the list right?

  • Well, yes you are right I am trying to stay fit.

  • Yes, lose a little weight too.

  • Ok yes my cholesterol has been a problem. But I'm medicating.

  • And for some reason that little smear of butter sounds so good right now.

  • Right.

  • Right.

  • Not even the cookie by itself?

  • You're right you're right I know you're right.

  • Pass the celery.
