GOOD SONGS THAT ARE REALLY MOSTLY JUST SOMEONE TALKING➕

Geoff Coffey
/ 21 May 2015

I’m a sucker for a story-song. They don’t even have to be singing.

  • Lake Marie / John Prine

    Perfect in the way only John Prine can be. Perfectly written and perfectly performed. http://bit.ly/1GrfnYJ

  • The Ballad of the Kingsmen / Todd Snider

    Possibly the greatest rant-song ever written. http://bit.ly/1Grfy6n

  • The Last Great American Whale / Lou Reed

    I hadn't listened to this song in years. It was on some compilation album I had in high school. A little preachy but still great. http://bit.ly/1ehe5o4

  • Nirvana / Tom Waits (Hat tip to Bukowski)

    This is really just Waits reciting a Bukowski poem so I'm not sure it belongs on this list, but it is so beautiful I included it anyway. http://bit.ly/1PXXPEf

  • Randal Knife / Guy Clark

    This was an iTunes Radio discovery and it hit me at the right moment. A little maudlin but powerful to me. http://bit.ly/1Grfunf

  • Blue / REM

    Lyrics only Michael Stipe could write. http://bit.ly/1Fyr7rv

  • A Most Disgusting Song / Sixto Rodriguez

    Beautifully real. http://bit.ly/1GriG1W

  • That Time / Regina Spektor

    The award for sudden tone change in the end. http://bit.ly/1Gsd9s4

  • Small Change / Tom Waits

    I could fill this list with Tom Waits but I'll end it with one of his best. http://bit.ly/1Hj6v3m
