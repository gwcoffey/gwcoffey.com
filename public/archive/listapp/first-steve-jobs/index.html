First Steve Jobs

Geoff Coffey
/ 10 August 2016

This is what I see at a glance with every “First Seven Jobs” list.

  1. First-ish highly successful personal computer.

  2. First commercially successful graphical user interface.

  3. First commercial failure.

  4. First computer-animated feature film.

  5. First major turnaround.

  6. First successful digital music player.

  7. First modern smartphone.
