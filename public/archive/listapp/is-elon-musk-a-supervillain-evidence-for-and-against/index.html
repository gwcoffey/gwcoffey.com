IS ELON MUSK A SUPERVILLAIN: EVIDENCE FOR AND AGAINST

Geoff Coffey
/ 4 October 2015

  • FOR: Apparently has access to unlimited nuclear bombs and the means to deliver them anywhere in the inner solar system.

  • AGAINST: Getting a little chubby.

  • FOR: Technological prowess seems to far surpass others.

  • AGAINST: Uses that prowess to make fast cars and batteries.

  • FOR: Billionaire

  • AGAINST: Made fortune from something with "Pal" in the name.

  • FOR: Named "Elon"

  • AGAINST: Named "Musk"

  • FOR: Has his fingers in diverse industries suitable for comprehensive take-over-the-world activities

  • AGAINST: Talks openly about plans and aspirations, gives away patent portfolio

  • FOR: Owns a space ship

  • AGAINST: Uses space ship to resupply science mission
