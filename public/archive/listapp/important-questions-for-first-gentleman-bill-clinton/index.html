Important Questions for First Gentleman Bill Clinton

Geoff Coffey
/ 27 July 2016

  • Will you be redecorating the presidential bedroom right away or do you plan to take your time?

  • Have you selected a color scheme?

  • Who designed your tie?

  • Have you been working out?

  • Do you really think bespoke tailored suites are the kind of thing ordinary Americans can identify with?

  • You've changed your hair!

    No question, just an observation.

  • Do you really think it is the place of someone in your position to dip into the political fray? Why not stay out of it and let your wife do her job?

  • What makes you qualified to talk about the American healthcare system? Or foreign policy?
