I would rather you read my diary than...

Geoff Coffey
/ 17 September 2016

  • See my YouTube history.

  • Ask me about a book when I've just finished.

  • Sit so close we have to touch on the airplane.

  • See me when I'm very sick.

  • Watch me eat junk food.

  • Pretend to know what I'm thinking.

  • See me cry.

  • Hear me share my feelings with someone I love.
