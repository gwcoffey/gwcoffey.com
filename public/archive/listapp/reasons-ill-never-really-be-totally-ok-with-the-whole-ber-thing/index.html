REASONS I'LL NEVER REALLY BE TOTALLY OK WITH THE WHOLE ÜBER THING

Geoff Coffey
/ 17 March 2016

  • Car sickness

    This is the main reason. Just took a lyft. Now I feel a little sick and I hate feeling a little sick.

  • Smells

    I am very sensitive to smells.

  • Social anxiety

    I mean it isn't all that bad but riding in a car with a stranger is kind of not fun.

  • I think Uber the company might be kind of evil

    Not sure. Hard to separate the truth from the faux internet outrage.

  • I have a car at home

    Although it is often being used by someone else I rarely need to go somewhere I can't walk.

  • I rent a car when I travel

    Most of my travel is for work and I always rent a car so ¯\_(ツ)_/¯
