THINGS BARBERS ALWAYS OVERESTIMATE

Geoff Coffey
/ 10 December 2015

  • Their ability to make my hair lay flat.

  • This has happened since I was a little boy.

  • "Can I put some gel in it?"

  • "More power to you man. I wish you luck."

  • I was basically Harry Potter.

  • Minus the wand, courage, and hot friend.

  • But with the hair and hand-me-down clothes.

  • Expecto patronum.
